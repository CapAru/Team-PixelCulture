Crafting Opportunities: Government and NGO Schemes for Local Artisans❤️

Amazon Karigar

An initiative by Amazon India, Karigar aims to showcase and promote authentic handcrafted products made by artisans across India. It provides artisans with a platform to sell their products online, reaching a wider audience and ensuring fair compensation for their work.

Learn More

National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP)

The NHDP is an attempt to facilitate the sustainable development of handloom weavers located in and outside identified handloom clusters into a cohesive, self managing and competitive socio-economic unit. NHDP has been formulated for its implementation during financial year 2021-22 to 2025-26.

Learn More

Aadi Mahotsav

Aadi Mahotsav, also known as the National Tribal Festival, is an annual event organized by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs. It celebrates the rich cultural heritage of tribal communities, showcasing their traditional crafts, art, music, and cuisine, and providing them with a platform to sell their products.
