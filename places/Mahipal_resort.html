Mahipal Resort

About

The Mahipal resort is one of the eco-resorts in the vicinity of the Mahipal dighi.A panoramic view of the panoramic beauty of Mahipal dighi, excavated by king Mahipal of Pal dynasty around 1100 years ago. The border between india and bangladesh is within 5 km of this resort. Ayera forest, a famous picnic spot, encircles the resort. It consists of mini-homes built on a high-rise platform. The resorts are equipped with balconies that provide panoramic views of the entire Dighi area.
