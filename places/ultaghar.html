Ulta Ghar

About

As the name suggests, everything is inverted in the house, right from its rooftop to the car, bed, air-conditioners, western commode, curtains and water taps. Even the floor is made of false ceiling that is below the roof in normal houses. Since its inauguration, the house has become the centre of attraction for tourists who visit Dooars. Ulta Ghar is located seven kilometres away from NH-31 that connects West Bengal to Assam.
