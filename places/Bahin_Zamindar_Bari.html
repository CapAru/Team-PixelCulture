Bahin Zamindar Bari

About

Located at Bahin Village, Raiganj block, Uttar Dinajpur, West Bengal. The house is situated on the banks of Nagar river, Raiganj. The house is one of the important historical places of North Dinajpur. The house is located at the border of the state of Bihar. The house was constructed by Bahin Zamindar Ishwar Chandra Chowdhury. The house is constructed of bricks. The house has large rooms and verandah. The house was declared as a heritage building by the West Bengal heritage commission in 2011.
