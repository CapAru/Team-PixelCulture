Annapurna Mandir

About

The Annapurna temple in Titagarh on the Ganga river bank is comparable to the Dakshineswar temple. On April 12, 1875 (30th Chaitra 1281), Sri Sri Ramkrishna Paramahansa opened it to devotees. Jagadamba Devi, the youngest daughter of Rani Rashmoni, constructed the temple. The magnificent temple is located close to Barrackpore on the Rani Rashmoni Ghat.
